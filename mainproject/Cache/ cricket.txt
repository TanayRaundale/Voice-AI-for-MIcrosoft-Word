#Slide: 2
#Header: What is Cricket?
#Content: Cricket is a popular sport played with a bat and ball. It originated in England and is now played in many countries around the world.

#Slide: 3
#Header: History of Cricket
#Content: Cricket has a rich history dating back to the 16th century. It evolved from a simple children's game to a professional sport with international competitions.

#Slide: 4
#Header: Rules and Gameplay
#Content: Cricket is played between two teams, with each team taking turns to bat and bowl. The objective is to score more runs than the opposing team.

#Slide: 5
#Header: Types of Cricket Matches
#Content: There are different formats of cricket, including Test matches, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20) matches, each with its own rules and duration.

#Slide: 6
#Header: Famous Cricket Players
#Content: Cricket has produced many legendary players, such as Sachin Tendulkar, Don Bradman, and Brian Lara, who have left a lasting impact on the sport.

#Slide: 7
#Header: Cricket World Cup
#Content: The Cricket World Cup is a prestigious tournament held every four years, where teams from different countries compete for the title of world champions.

#Slide: 8
#Header: Conclusion
#Content: Cricket is a captivating sport with a rich history and a global following. It combines skill, strategy, and teamwork, making it a beloved game for millions of fans worldwide.
